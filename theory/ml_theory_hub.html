How these relate
Data engineering moves and shapes data; classical ML often runs on tabular features in a warehouse; deep learning learns representations from raw signals; AI engineering ships and monitors models; RAG and fine-tuning specialize LLMs. DevOps enables reliable delivery; MLOps and LLMOps add ML- and LLM-specific automation on top.
